Transit SIOP

I spent this past summer as a UI/UX designer and front-end developer in a team of 6 interns researching, designing, and development a redesigned webapp for one of Wabtec's internal SIOP applications.

What I worked on

As the UX designer and front-end developer working within a balanced development team, I had a wonderful experience working and collaborating with engineering counterparts and endusers to design and implement a deliverable to Transit SIOP, which is a sales forcasting tool. I worked on providing the business analysts and functional users with a single page app layout where they would be able to seamlessly move from page to page and form to form.
