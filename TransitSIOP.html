Internal Sales Forecasting Application

Organization: Wabtec Corporation

Team: Jaylynn Johnson, Dan Mezhinsky, Cole Dombrowski

Role: UX Engineer

Design Tools: Figma

Development Tools: Angular

This past summer, I was part of an intern team that was tasked with redesigning and developing a web-application for one of Wabtec's internal sales forecasting applications.

At Wabtec, the internal sales applications use various parameters and inputs for projecting the sales that would be generated from parameters and incoming projects that are gained by the company.

Previously, the application was developed with Microsoft Access, PowerBI, and the company's Data Mart. The issue that users and stakeholders were running into is that the app was expensive in terms of memory and cost and that the user interface was not intuitive and did not cater to functional users among other pain points. My project goal was to provide the functional users with visibility into the projections without having an information overload. My team and I developed single page app layout where they could be seamlessly move from page to page and form to form while having the powerBI projections available on the same page.
