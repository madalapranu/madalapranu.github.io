Scanning Application for Shipment Reciept Transaction Processing

Date: May 2024 - July 2024

Team: Allison Flat, Hayden Fisher, Rachel Mathew, Cassidy Como

Role: UX Engineer and Project Manager

Design Tools: Figma

Development Tools: XML, Java, Postman, Android Studio

The Wabtec site in Piossasco, Italy, is updating its older ERP system by migrating to Oracle for better efficiency in processing shipment transactions. As part of this upgrade, the site will use new processing devices to scan barcodes and QR codes, allowing operators to quickly access shipment details and approve or reject lines, with the accepted data being sent directly to Oracle.

As the team's UI/UX designer, my goal was to provide the site's shopfloor operators the scanning capability, display the requesting packing slip information and receipt details, and intuitive screen navigation without cluttering up the Zebra Scanner's screen space.
