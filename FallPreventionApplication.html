Fall Prevention

A mobile application designed to assist healthcare clinicians, older adults, and individuals with cognitive impairments in planning and achieving fall prevention goal.


Organization: Vertically Integrated Projects @ Georgia Tech

Team: Brain Trauma Assessment Protocols

Role:
  • Administrator Portal UX Engineer
  • Project Manager

Design Tools: Figma

Development Tools:
  • ReactJS
  • TypeScript


Identify

We conducted a interview via an email chain with our client to better understand what she was looking for in the administrator portal of the Fall Prevention Application. From the conversation, we were able to gather that she wanted 3 major functions to appear: Login capability, New User Account feature, and the ability to track the performance of their respective patients. The images below dive deeper details on each function.


Ideate

Over the course of one month, I took the user requirements that we were given and used Figma to create the mockup of the web application. Because the iOS application has a color scheme of blues and whites, I decided to stick to that color palette to maintain consistency across the platforms. The images displayed show the primary screens for the web application.


Development

To develop the web-application, I am using the ReactJS framework and TypeScript. Currently the website is under-development and I will be workign on this over the course of the next 2-3 months.

User Testing

We conducted user testing of the web application by sending our client the mockups that were created, and got some insight into her expectations for the end result. The image below shows some of the feedback we received.
