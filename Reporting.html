Designing a new Landing Page for Production Data at Wabtec

Date: May 2024 - July 2024

Team: MES and Smart MFG

Role: UX Engineer

Design Tools: Figma

Project Description: Wabtec uses a homegrown tool called OpsVision for monitoring, collecting, and displaying data from their MES applications. Over the years, various developers created over 50+ site specific reports, which led to a landing page that is dated in terms of user navigation.

My role: My goal was to create a new landing page that was very easy to use for both new and existing users. After creating several mockup options, I came up with a layout that is similar to Google Drive, where users would have access to a search engine, and they would be able to see their frequently used reports upon coming to the page.
